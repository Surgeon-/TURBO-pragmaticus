
Things you can do with Focus:
    - Take 1 additional die for a skill test by FORKing (1 Focus)
    - Use early Effort (1 Focus)
    - Use late Effort (1 Focus)
    - Apply a Perk to a skill test (1 Focus unless stated otherwise)
    - Receive aid for a skill test from another character (1 Focus)
    
Things that decrease Focus:
    - Wounds
    - Stress
